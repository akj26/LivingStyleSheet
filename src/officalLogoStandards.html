DID(it) FORMATTING

The lettering in the DID(it) should always be uppercase for ‘DID’ and lowercase for (it), and always with the parentheses around ‘it’ in all communications including paragraph text.

WHITESPACE

When using the logo, it’s recommended it be on a white background, and the field around it should be white with at least 20 pixels of white space on all sides. If used on a dark field, the entire logo should be white. If placed over a photo or pattern background, use a circle around the logo with white background and aqua or blue stroke (see examples).

PLACEMENT

When possible it should be centered and at the top or bottom of all communications, with the USCIS-DHS seal in the upper left hand corner so it’s more prominent (the first item, reading top left to right).
